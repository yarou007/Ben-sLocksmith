About Us

Commercial Glass Experts
Who We Are

Licensed, insured commercial glaziers serving Washington DC, Northern Virginia, Maryland, and New York since 2010. We specialize exclusively in commercial glass — no residential work.

Our Story

Commercial Glass Experts was founded in 2010 by a team of licensed glaziers who'd spent years working for larger glass companies and recognized that commercial clients weren't getting the specialized service they needed. Residential glass companies taking commercial calls. Slow response times. Lack of insurance documentation expertise. We set out to build a company that treated commercial glass as the specialized, time-sensitive service it is.

Today we're one of the most trusted commercial glass companies in the DC metro area — with service teams positioned throughout Washington DC, Northern Virginia, Maryland, and New York for rapid emergency response.

What Makes Us Different

  • Commercial only — Every job is commercial. Our technicians work exclusively on commercial glass systems and know them deeply.
  • Response time commitment — We track our average response time publicly: under 45 minutes to any location in our core service area.
  • Insurance documentation specialists — We've processed thousands of commercial glass insurance claims. We know what adjusters need.
  • Licensed in every jurisdiction we serve — DC DCRA, Virginia DPOR, Maryland DLLR, New York DOB. All current, all fully insured.
