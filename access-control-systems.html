Access Control Systems

Control who enters and when — without managing piles of keys. We install and repair commercial access control systems, electrified locks, and entry hardware for offices, retail, and multi-tenant buildings.

🔑 703-244-0559 Access Control Service

Systems & Hardware We Work With

  • Keycard & fob readers — common area doors, staff-only doors, back-of-house entries
  • Keypad entry — PIN access for small offices and secure rooms
  • Electric strikes — controlled entry while maintaining latch security
  • Maglocks — commercial installs with proper egress and life-safety considerations
  • Intercoms & door buzzers — visitor access and remote release
  • Door position & request-to-exit devices — safer, more reliable controlled egress

Common Reasons Businesses Upgrade

  • Staff turnover — disable credentials instead of changing keys
  • Multiple entrances — consistent access rules across doors
  • After-hours access — secure entries without handing out master keys
  • Audit readiness — improved control over who has access

Pair With Mechanical Key Control

Many sites use access control for primary doors and a mechanical system for secondary doors. If you need both, we can coordinate access control with a master key system and overall security upgrade plan.
