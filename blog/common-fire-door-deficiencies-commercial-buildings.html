Common Fire Door Deficiencies in Commercial Buildings

The most frequent failures are latching problems, closer issues, clearance violations, and non-compliant hardware changes. These are usually fixable with targeted correction work.

Call 703-244-0559Deficiency Repair Service

Top Deficiencies

  • Door does not self-latch from open position
  • Door closer leaking, broken, or poorly adjusted
  • Excessive gaps at head/jamb/undercut
  • Missing or damaged labels on rated assemblies
  • Improper hardware substitutions after prior repairs
  • Damaged frame alignment or hinge wear
  • Exit devices and panic bars not operating correctly

Why These Deficiencies Repeat

Many properties run reactive maintenance only. Without inspection-driven correction planning, the same openings fail repeatedly each year.
