Door Closer Problems and Fire Door Compliance

If a closer on a rated opening leaks, slams, or does not close and latch, the condition may be cited during an applicable inspection. The opening's listing, approved design, inspection scope, and AHJ direction determine the finding.

Warning Signs

  • Oil leaking from closer body
  • Door slams into frame
  • Door stalls and will not latch
  • Closer arm bent or loose
  • Door drifts open after traffic cycles
  • Uneven close speed from different open angles

Record these signs for evaluation against the opening's listing, approved design, and applicable inspection scope.

Quick Property Manager Actions

  • Tag and log failing openings
  • Prioritize doors with heavy egress use
  • Schedule authorized correction before the property's required inspection
  • Document adjustments and replacements
