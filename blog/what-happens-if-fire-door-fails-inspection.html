What Happens If a Fire Door Fails Inspection?

Next steps depend on the written findings and responsible inspector or AHJ. The report may require correction, documentation, temporary safeguards, or follow-up review; a service contractor cannot declare closeout unless expressly authorized.

Potential Next Steps

  • Correction, documentation, or follow-up identified in the written findings
  • Operational pressure for high-traffic doors
  • Direction on temporary safeguards or operating restrictions, where applicable
  • Scheduling strain for property and maintenance teams
  • Potential follow-up review timelines

Use the written findings and responsible authority's direction to set priorities, document authorized work, and arrange any required follow-up.

Immediate Next Steps

  • Prioritize doors with active egress/latching failure
  • Bundle corrections by building zone
  • Document every corrected item clearly
  • Prepare for follow-up verification
