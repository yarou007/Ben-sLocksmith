Commercial Glass Repair in New York City

New York City is the most demanding commercial glass market in the country. The density of commercial storefronts, the 24/7 business environment, and the strict NYC DOB requirements make commercial glazing in the five boroughs a specialized service requiring licensed, experienced glaziers.

We hold NY commercial glazing contractor licensing and are familiar with NYC Department of Buildings requirements, Local Law compliance, and the specific challenges of working in Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Queens — limited street access, freight elevator logistics, co-op and condo building management approvals, and after-hours work windows.
