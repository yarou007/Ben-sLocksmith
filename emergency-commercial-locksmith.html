Emergency Commercial Locksmith

Locked out of your business, dealing with a broken lock, or can’t secure the door after-hours? We dispatch 24/7 for commercial emergencies — with non-destructive entry whenever possible.

Emergency Scenarios We Handle

  • Business lockouts — office, retail storefront, warehouse, back-of-house doors
  • Broken or seized locks — key stuck, cylinder failure, mortise lock issues
  • Urgent re-key requests — compromised keys, staff turnover, missing keys
  • Door won’t secure — latch/strike misalignment, hardware failure, closers not latching
  • Exit door emergencies — panic bar not latching, dogging failure, trim issues

What to Expect When You Call

  • Live dispatch — no voicemail; we confirm location and door type
  • Fast arrival — average response under 45 minutes in our core service area
  • Commercial-grade fixes — the goal is a secure, code-compliant door, not a temporary workaround
  • Clear options — re-key vs. repair vs. upgrade (with upfront estimates)

Need a Non-Emergency Upgrade?

If the emergency is tied to key control or recurring lockouts, we can help you prevent the next incident with a master key system or access control system.
