Fire Door Inspection Checklist

  • Rated labels and hardware compatibility
  • Self-closing and positive latching behavior
  • Frame condition and door alignment
  • Clearances, seals, and undercut checks
  • Panic bar / exit device operation
  • Closer speed, backcheck, and latch action
  • Opening condition notes and deficiency IDs
