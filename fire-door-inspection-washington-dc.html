Fire Door Inspection Checklist

  • Door and frame labels are present and legible
  • No field modifications that void fire rating
  • Door self-closes and latches from full open position
  • Clearances at head, jamb, and undercut are compliant
  • Glazing, vision kits, and seals are intact
  • Panic bars and exit devices operate correctly
  • Door closer speed and latch action are adjusted
  • Hinges, strikes, and latch points are secure
