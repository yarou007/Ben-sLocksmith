Commercial Door Closer Service

Door closers are one of the most common failure points on commercial doors. When a closer is out of adjustment or worn out, it can cause safety issues, security gaps, and code problems. We service surface-mounted and concealed closers and can match replacements to your door and traffic needs.

Common Door Closer Problems

  • Door slams shut or closes too fast
  • Door won’t close all the way or won’t latch
  • Closer is leaking oil or the arm is loose
  • Door drifts open or won’t stay closed
  • Backcheck not working or opening is too heavy

Repair, Adjustment, and Replacement

We can tune sweep speed, latch speed, and backcheck, replace arms and brackets, and swap failed closers with reliable commercial-grade models. If your door is a fire-rated opening, we help ensure the door self-closes and latches properly.
