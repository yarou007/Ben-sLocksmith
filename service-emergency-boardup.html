What Is Emergency Commercial Board-Up?

Emergency board-up is the immediate securing of broken commercial glass using OSB (oriented strand board) or plywood panels, fastened into existing window and door frames. It's a temporary measure — typically lasting until permanent glass arrives in 24–72 hours — but it must be done correctly to be effective.

Commercial board-up requires different techniques than residential. Commercial aluminum frames require specific fastening hardware to avoid further damage. Large commercial panels need reinforced boarding. Our technicians are trained specifically for commercial applications.

What to Do When Your Glass Is Broken

If you're dealing with a commercial glass emergency right now:

  • Call us immediately: 703-244-0559. Live dispatcher, no voicemail, 24/7.
  • Don't enter if unsafe: Broken commercial glass, especially tempered glass, can be dangerous. Wait for our tech.
  • Document what you can: If safe, take photos before anything is moved — this helps your insurance claim.
  • Contact your insurer: Notify your commercial property insurer. We'll provide the documentation they need.

The Board-Up Process: What to Expect

When our tech arrives:

  • Safety assessment — area secured, sharp glass fragments cleared
  • Damage photographed and documented for insurance
  • OSB panels cut to fit and installed into existing frames using commercial-grade fasteners
  • Panels weatherproofed and secured against unauthorized entry
  • Written damage report and insurance estimate provided before we leave
  • Permanent glass ordered immediately for next-day or same-day installation where possible
