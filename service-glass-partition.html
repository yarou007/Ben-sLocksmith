Commercial Glass Partitions & Office Glass Walls

Glass partition walls transform commercial office spaces — improving light flow, maintaining visual connection, and creating a modern professional environment. We design and install glass partition systems for offices, co-working spaces, and commercial interiors throughout the region.

Types of Commercial Glass Partition Systems

We install three main categories of glass partition systems:

  • Fully frameless glass partitions — Floor-to-ceiling glass panels with minimal or no visible framing. Maximum transparency, cleanest aesthetic. Requires structural support engineering.
  • Semi-frameless systems — Glass panels with thin aluminum top tracks and occasional vertical posts. Good balance of openness and structural simplicity.
  • Aluminum-framed systems — Traditional commercial partition framing with 3" or 4" aluminum posts and rails. Most cost-effective, widest glass size flexibility.

We also install movable/demountable partition systems — fully removable glass walls that can be reconfigured as your space needs change.

Glass Options for Office Partitions

Glass selection matters as much as the framing system. Common choices:

  • Clear tempered glass — Maximum transparency. 1/2" or 3/8" tempered for floor-to-ceiling applications.
  • Frosted or etched glass — Privacy without full opacity. Conference rooms, executive offices, HR areas.
  • Tinted glass — Reduces glare and solar gain while maintaining transparency. Good for south and west-facing office walls.
  • Smart glass / switchable privacy glass — Electronically switchable between clear and frosted. Premium option for executive spaces and conference rooms.
  • Acoustic glass — Laminated glass that reduces sound transmission. STC ratings from 35 to 50+ depending on configuration.

Installation Process

Glass partition installation is a clean, low-disruption process compared to traditional construction. We work around occupied office schedules — evenings and weekends available — and our installations typically require no wet work (no drywall, no painting). Timeline from measurement to installed:

  • Site measurement and design consultation: 1–2 hours
  • Shop drawings and approval: 3–5 business days
  • Glass fabrication: 5–10 business days
  • Installation (typical 1,000 sq ft open office build-out): 2–4 days
