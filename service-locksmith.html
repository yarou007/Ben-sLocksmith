Re-Key vs. New Locks

Re-keying changes the internal pin configuration to work with a new key — the hardware stays in place. It's almost always the right choice after employee termination or key loss. New locks are appropriate when hardware is worn, doesn't meet current code, or needs a security upgrade. We recommend re-keying as standard practice on every commercial tenant change.
